# Partnership

Cluster Terminal is an actively developing professional trading platform. We are open to collaboration and looking for partners to help grow the project.

We are seeking investors to support:

- Over 50 order flow indicators, 62 drawing tools, real-time streaming data
- Growing user base in the CIS and European crypto trading community
- Fully self-hosted infrastructure with low operational costs
- Active development with regular releases
